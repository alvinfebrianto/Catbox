# FAQ

---

## What is the maximum file size I can upload?

The maximum file size for an unregistered user is 10MB, while the maximum for a registered user is 30MB.

## What file types can be uploaded?

Jpg/Jpeg, Png, Gif, and Webp are allowed for anyone. Registered users can also upload Mp4 videos.

## Can I use HTML, BBCode, or markdown in my descriptions?

Image descriptions may use markdown.

## How long do you keep files?

Files are currently kept forever. We will notify users via e-mail if the need changes in the future.

## How do I get my post on the front page?

After creating a post, you can publish your post to the community. Published posts are able to be browsed by others and will show up on the front page.

## How many files can I upload at once?

Unregistered users may not currently upload content.
Registered users can upload 20 files at one time.
Gold members can upload 50 at once.
If you need more, you can add them to the post once it is created.

## Can I delete a post after I create it?

The menu on the right side of your post will have a delete option as long as you just created it, or if you created it under a registered account and are logged in to that account. A deletion URL is provided for any post as well, if you'd like to save it in case you'd like to delete a post at a later time. This is especially helpful for users without accounts.

## What is the difference between the post statuses?

- **Public** — can be viewed by anyone, is available via search, and will show up on the front page.
- **Hidden** — can only be accessed via the url, it will not show up in search or be on the front page.
- **Password Protected** — can only be accessed by the user that created it or via a password set by the creator. It does not show up in search or the front page.
- **Secret** — can only be accessed by the user that created it. It does not show up in search or the front page.

## How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the [account section of your profile](https://imgchest.com/profile/account). Once you re-enter your password and confirm deletion, you will be logged out and your account queued for deletion.

## What are Experience Points (XP)?

XP reflects your activity and growth on Image Chest. Every action, like posting, commenting, or favoriting, helps you earn XP and showcase your engagement with the community.

## How do I earn XP?

You can gain XP in several ways:

- **Engagement** — Posts with lots of views, comments, and favorites boost your XP.
- **Community Interaction** — Commenting on and favoriting posts, as well as participating in discussions, adds to your XP.

## What are Accolades?

Accolades are special achievements that recognize your milestones and participation. They come in two types:

- **Regular Accolades** — earned through normal activities like uploading your first post or reaching milestones.
- **Secret Accolades** — hidden achievements unlocked by completing specific challenges or actions.

## How do I view my XP and Accolades?

Your XP and accolades are displayed on your profile, allowing you to track your progress and share your achievements with the community.

## Do post and favorite folders have limits?

Yes, there are limits on the number of post and favorite folders you can create, and they differ based on your membership:

- **Regular members** — up to 5 folders
- **Gold members** — up to 20 folders

## How do I rename a folder?

When you create a folder it is created immediately with the name of "New Folder". To rename it, select the folder, then click on its name to begin the process.

## What are the rules for uploading an avatar?

To keep things fast, secure, and looking great across devices, we have a few requirements for avatars:

- **File types allowed:** JPG, JPEG, PNG, or WEBP
- **Size limit:** 2MB max
- **Image dimensions:** Must be at least 100x100 pixels and no larger than 1000x1000 pixels

**Optional:** Uploading an avatar is completely optional.

If your image is too large or the wrong type, try resizing or converting it before uploading.
